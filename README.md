# Varinda Store – Annual Sales Analysis (2022)

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Varinda Store’s sales performance for the year 2022. The analysis is performed using Excel to extract meaningful insights from order, customer, product, and sales data.

The goal of this project is to identify key business trends, customer behavior, and performance patterns to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance for 2022  
- Identify top-performing months, states, and sales channels  
- Understand customer demographics and purchasing behavior  
- Evaluate order status distribution  
- Identify best-selling product categories  
- Provide actionable business insights  

---

## Dataset
The analysis is based on the following datasets:
- Orders Data  
- Customers Data  
- Products Data  
- Sales Transactions Data  

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Top Performing Months
- January, February, and March recorded the highest sales and order volume  
- Strong performance in Q1 indicates seasonal demand trends  

### 2. Customer Demographics
- Women contributed 64% of total purchases  
- Men contributed 36% of total purchases  
- Majority of customers belong to the female segment  

### 3. Order Status Analysis
- 92% orders were successfully delivered  
- 2% refunded  
- 3% returned  
- 3% cancelled  
- High operational efficiency observed  

### 4. Top Performing States
- Maharashtra  
- Karnataka  
- Uttar Pradesh  
These states contributed the highest sales volume  

### 5. Age Group & Gender Analysis
- Women aged 30–49 years were the most active buyers  
- This segment represents the core customer base  

### 6. Sales Channel Contribution
- Amazon  
- Myntra  
- Flipkart  
These platforms generated the highest revenue share  

### 7. Best-Selling Categories
- Set  
- Kurta  
- Western Dress  
High demand observed in women’s apparel segment  

---

## Conclusion
The analysis shows that Varinda Store performs strongly among women aged 30–49 years, with major contributions from top Indian states and leading e-commerce platforms.

To improve future performance, the business should focus on:
- Targeted marketing for high-value customer segments  
- Improving return and refund processes  
- Strengthening top-performing sales channels  
- Expanding high-demand product categories
